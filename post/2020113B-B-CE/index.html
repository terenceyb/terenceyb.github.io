2020年11月三笔 B卷 C-E

作者: 原驳 日期: 2021-01-22 阅读时间:3.2 分钟 字数:733 分类: CATTI真题

主 题 ：杂 交 水 稻，造 福 世 界
难 度 ：★★★☆☆
出 处 ：不 详

1

水稻是世界上最主要的粮食作物之一，世界一半以上人口（包括中国60%以上人口）都以稻米作为主食。中国是世界上最早种植水稻的国家，至今已有7000年左右的历史，当前水稻产量占全国粮食作物产量近一半。

参考译文：

As one of the most majoring crops, rice feeds half of the population in the world (including 60% of Chinese) as the staple food. China is the very early country to grow rice with a history of 7,000 years. Currently, the rice production makes up half of the total grain production in China.

2

水稻作为主要的粮食，无论对中国还是对世界的重要性都是不言而喻的。中国在超级杂交水稻（Super hybrid rice）生产方面成就突出，关键人物便是袁隆平。被誉为"中国杂交水稻之父"。他的名字不仅在中国家喻户晓，在世界上也享有盛誉。

参考译文：

Therefore, rice is well-known to be an important major crop not only in China but also in the world. China has been achieved greatly in super hybrid rice cultivation with its core Yuan Longping who is reputed as "Father of China's Hybrid Rice". He is widely known in China and enjoys a global reputation.

3

袁隆平于上世纪60年代开始杂交水稻研究。他带领的研究团队使中国的杂交水稻一直领先于世界水平，不仅不断实现杂交水稻的高产量目标，而且在生产实践中不断推广应用，从实际上解决了中国人吃饭难的问题。袁隆平还多次到美国、印度等国家传授技术，为30多个国家和地区的政府官员和科研工作者讲学，促进杂交水稻技术造福世界。

参考译文：

Yuan started his researches since 1960s and led his team making China hybrid rice to a world leading level, which accomplished the high production and popularized its application. Therefore, the problem of grain shortage has been solved. Yuan also visited the US and India many times for technology spreading and gave lectures to officials and scientific researchers from more than 30 countries and areas, boosting the technology of hybrid rice benefits people around the world.

4

1987年11月3日，联合国教科文组织在巴黎总部向袁隆平颁发科学奖，认为他的科研成果是"第二次绿色革命"。2004年，袁隆平获得世界粮食奖（the World Food Prize），表彰他为人类提供营养丰富、数量充足的粮食所作出的突出贡献。

参考译文：

On November 3, 1987, UNESCO honored Yuan with Science Prize at its headquarters in Paris and took his achievements "the Second Green Revolution". In 2004, he won the World Food Prize for his great contribution which provides nutritious and abundant foods to mankind.
